A Moment of Shooting

Against a vivid orange background, this design combines the dynamic posture of a basketball player shooting with letter elements like "EXCIT", and features the phrase "JUST DO IT" below. Through color impact and the capture of the player’s movement, the overall design showcases the passion and vitality of basketball, conveying the spirit of daring to act and enjoying the moment of sports.
